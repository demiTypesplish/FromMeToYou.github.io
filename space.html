Supernova: bursting into brilliance, too beautiful for the darkness to contain

Understanding the tragedy of a supernova reminded me that the stars spend a lifetime hoarding light and gathering elements, only to burst it all out in a single, beautiful moment of brilliant explosion.
The light from a supernova can outshine an entire galaxy for a brief period, illuminating the cosmos with its radiant glow.
Then, I found myself in this scenario, gathering all the feelings, affections, and every bit of admiration until my core couldn't handle it anymore, and I burst into a supernova of emotions, shining brightly. With the hopes of my light reaching this star I admire.
You are the star. You shine so luminous that I want to reach you, and for a moment, I'll be able to be a part of your brilliance.
Let the cosmos bow before your celestial radiance, my luminous starlight, for your light eclipses the heavens, leaving the proudest stars faint from your beauty.
